Everything you need to know before you get started.

Most teams are up and running in under 30 minutes. You can import your existing leads via CSV, connect your lead sources, and start managing your pipeline right away. We also offer a guided onboarding flow that walks you through every step.

No credit card required to start your 14-day free trial. You get full access to all Professional features during the trial. We'll only ask for payment details when you decide to upgrade.

Lead Buket integrates with WhatsApp Business, Google Ads, Facebook Lead Ads, Zapier, Slack, Gmail, Outlook, Mailchimp, HubSpot, and 50+ more tools. Enterprise plans include custom API access and webhooks for any integration you need.
